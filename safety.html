Important notice for your safety!

In the past criminals have tried to impersonate me by using fake accounts or domains
to arbitrarily contact people and get their credit card information by offering fake discounts or coupons for my releases.


Therefore, I have created some basic rules that will help protecting you from phishing attacks.


Rule #1

I will never send unsolicited private messages to individual people saying that the offer is very limited in time or extremely cheap.


Rule #2

I will never send private messages to individuals to follow, subscribe or like a profile on any social network.
Real accounts of mine on social networks are always linked on the home page.


Rule #3

I will never ever ask for your credit card information or other payment methods like PayPal, PaySafeCard
or gift cards from services like Amazon, Apple or Google.


Rule #4

I will never ask for personal information such as name, address and date of birth.


Rule #5

I will never author sale contracts personally or directly. Especially not via private messages.
If you want to purchase or stream my releases, you have to use the links on the start page and choose the service you like.


Rule #6

I will never advertise my releases on other profiles, comments, topics or social networks that are not clearly linked.


Rule #7

I will never use URL shortener services like bit.ly or goo.gl for my products or services.
These URL shorteners are often used to hide phishing domains and make it difficult for victims to validate the authenticity.
