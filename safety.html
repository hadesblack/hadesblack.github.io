HOMESOCIAL

Important notice for your safety!

In the past, criminals have tried to impersonate me using fake accounts or domains
to contact people and get their credit card information by offering fake discounts or coupons for my releases.


Therefore, I have created some basic rules that will help protecting you from phishing attacks.


Rule #1

I will never send unsolicited private messages to individual people claiming that the offer is very limited in time or very cheap.


Rule #2

I will never send private messages to individuals prompting to follow, subscribe or like a profile on any social network.
Real accounts from me on social networks are always linked on the home page.


Rule #3

I will never ever ask for your credit card information or other payment methods like PayPal
or gift cards from services like Amazon, Apple or Google.


Rule #4

I will never ask for personal information such as name, home address, phone number, email address or date of birth.


Rule #5

I will never author sale contracts personally or directly. Especially not via private messages.
If you want to purchase or stream my releases, you have to use the links on the home page and choose the service you like.


Rule #6

I will never advertise my releases, profiles or other products on other profiles, comments, topics or social networks that are not clearly linked on the home page.


Rule #7

I will never use URL shortener services like "bit.ly" or "goo.gl" for my releases, products or social network profiles.
These URL shorteners are often used to hide phishing domains / accounts and make it difficult for victims to validate the authenticity.
